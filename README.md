# GoPinion Outbound OS

A modular outbound operating system for finding leads, enriching companies and contacts, running outreach, booking appointments, and learning from outcomes.

## Initial ICP

GoPinion Outbound OS starts with independent used car dealers in the U.S.

The ideal early customer profile is:

- 11-25 cars sold per month
- Has a website and an active Google Business Profile
- Google rating around 3.2-4.0
- 15-150 total reviews
- Low or inconsistent review response habits
- Growth-oriented, pain-aware, or solution-aware
- Likely owner or operator age range of 38-52
- Already uses at least 2 SaaS tools

The team should avoid prioritizing:

- Dealers with no website and no Google Business Profile
- Dealers under 5 cars per month
- Accounts already locked into Birdeye or Podium
- Dealers with 4.8+ stars and no visible pain
- Large franchise or corporate dealer groups
- Highly tech-resistant or retiring operators

## Initial Offers

- Reviews / Reputation
- NAPS

## Main Goal

Book 5 appointments per day.

## V1 Focus

Version 1 should support:

- Lead intake
- Company and contact enrichment
- Decision-maker inference
- Lead scoring
- Outreach sequencing
- Reply classification
- Booking
- Insights
- Learning from outcomes

Keep the product modular, use placeholder integrations where needed, and optimize for clean foundations over speed hacks.
